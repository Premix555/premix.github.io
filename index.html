Pomodoro Timer

The Pomodoro study technique is designed to improve focus by breaking work into intervals. The inspiration for hardware implementation is to do standalone study session without mobile phone (there are pomodoro software app). The timer counts 20 minutes for study time, followed by a 5-minute break. The design features a user-triggered counter with buttons and buzzers, utilizing an STM32 microcontroller and a 7-segment display.
